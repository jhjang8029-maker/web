The email you clicked was a simulated phishing message designed to improve security awareness.

Common signs of phishing emails include:

  • Urgent requests (e.g., "Action required within 24 hours")
  • Requests to reset passwords or log in
  • Suspicious or shortened links
  • Sender address different from official domain
  • Grammar mistakes or unusual wording

If you receive a suspicious email, do not click links or open attachments. Always verify through your IT security team or official channels.
